02 — Portable

Clover Flex

Clover Flex is your solution for accepting credit cards on-the-go. Processing via both Wifi or cellular data, Clover Flex allows you to accept cards just about anywhere! You also get all the business-building features that Clover is known for, the ability to add optional applications to customize your system, and cloud-based data storage.

Features
Wifi and cellular connectivity
Accept multiple credit and debit types
EMV (chip + PIN; chip + signature)
Contactless (including Apple Pay)
Swipe (MSR) & PIN Debit
Barcode scanner & front-facing camera
7″ Antimicrobial Corning® Gorilla® Glass
Lasts all day on a single charge

Clover Mini

A compact countertop POS with the guts to go big. The Clover Mini is as powerful as it is sleek — and the lightweight footprint won't hog your counter space, or any workspace for that matter.
